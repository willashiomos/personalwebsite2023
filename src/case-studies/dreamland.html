Case study

Dreamland

Web Design | UI Design | Branding

Design an online candle shop that is both stylish and uncomplicated, appealing to both aesthetic cohesion and ease of use.

Design principles

  • Clean - show and say things in the simplest way possible without sacrificing style or cohesion
  • Universal - design for human beings with different wants and needs
  • Cohesive - each part of the system aligns with the whole and contributes to the larger system

Process

I began by wireframing the site experience.

I then developed a design system to fit the objective and principles.

Part of this project involved developing a chat bot flow and interaction.
